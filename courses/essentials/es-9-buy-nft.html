Buy an NFT on fractal.is

Browse the largest Cardano NFT marketplace, find something you like, and buy it — all in a few minutes with your Lace wallet.

⏱ 4 min 🖼 ADA in your Lace wallet required 🔑 No account needed
Home › Courses › Essentials › Buy an NFT
🖼 What you'll do: Buy your first Cardano NFT
💰 Cost: Floor price of your chosen collection + ~2 ADA fee
⚠️ Note: NFT values can go to zero — only buy what you like
1

Go to fractal.is

Type fractal.is directly into your browser. This is the largest NFT marketplace on Cardano. You can browse all collections without connecting your wallet first.

📸 Screenshot: fractal.is homepage Show the fractal.is homepage with trending collections visible
2

Browse collections and find one you like

Click Collections to see all available NFT projects sorted by volume, floor price, or recency. You can filter by price range. Click any collection to see individual items for sale.

⚠️

Most NFTs are speculative assets whose value can drop to zero. Only buy what you genuinely like or find interesting — don't buy purely hoping to sell for profit. This is not financial advice.

📸 Screenshot: fractal.is — Collections page Show the collection grid with floor price and volume displayed on each card
3

Connect your Lace wallet

When you're ready to buy, click Connect Wallet in the top right corner and select Lace. Approve the connection in your Lace extension.

📸 Screenshot: fractal.is wallet connection popup Show the wallet selector with Lace highlighted and the Authorize button
4

Select an NFT and click "Buy Now"

Find an item listed for sale (it will show a price and a Buy Now button). Click it to open the listing detail. Check the price in ADA, then click Buy Now.

📸 Screenshot: fractal.is — NFT listing detail page Show the NFT image, price in ADA, and the "Buy Now" button
5

Confirm the purchase in Lace

Your Lace wallet opens with a transaction showing the NFT price plus a small platform fee (~2%) and network fee. Review the amounts, click Confirm, and enter your wallet password.

📸 Screenshot: Lace — NFT purchase transaction confirmation Show the transaction with the ADA amount, fee breakdown, and Confirm button
✅

Your NFT will appear in your Lace wallet under the NFTs tab within a minute. It's stored on-chain — you own it fully, no platform can take it away.
