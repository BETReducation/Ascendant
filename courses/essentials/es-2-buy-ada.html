Buy ADA and move it to your wallet

Create an exchange account, buy ADA with regular money, then send it to your own Lace wallet. Step by step.

⏱ 8 min to read 💳 Bank transfer or card required 🔑 You need your Lace wallet ready first
Home › Courses › Essentials › Buy ADA
✅ What you'll have: ADA in your own Lace wallet
🏦 What you'll need: A bank account and photo ID
⏳ Heads up: ID verification can take 10–30 minutes
1

Choose a reputable exchange

You need an exchange to turn your regular money (£, $, €) into ADA. Good options available in most countries include Kraken, Coinbase, and Binance. All three are regulated, support ADA, and allow withdrawals to your own wallet.

⚠️

Always check the exchange is available in your country and supports ADA withdrawals before signing up. Some exchanges only let you trade — not withdraw to your own wallet.

📸 Screenshot: Kraken or Coinbase homepage Show the exchange homepage with a "Sign up" or "Get started" button
2

Create your account and verify your identity

Click Sign Up, enter your email, choose a strong password, and follow the prompts. All regulated exchanges require identity verification (called KYC). Have your passport or driving licence ready — you'll be asked to take a photo of it.

💡 Use a password you don't use anywhere else. Consider a password manager like 1Password or Bitwarden.
📸 Screenshot: Exchange sign-up form Show the email and password fields on the registration page
3

Enable two-factor authentication (2FA)

Before depositing any money, go to your account Security settings and enable 2FA. Download an authenticator app (Google Authenticator or Authy), scan the QR code shown, and enter the 6-digit code to confirm.

⚠️

Do not skip this step. An exchange account without 2FA can be taken over with just your email and password. 2FA means a hacker needs your physical phone too.

📸 Screenshot: Exchange security settings — 2FA setup Show the QR code screen and the 6-digit code entry field
4

Deposit money to your account

Go to Deposit or Add funds in your exchange account. Choose your currency (GBP, USD, EUR etc.) and select Bank transfer — it's usually free, though it takes 1–2 working days. Card deposits are instant but carry a 1–3% fee.

📸 Screenshot: Exchange deposit page Show the currency selection and deposit method options (bank transfer vs card)
5

Search for ADA and buy

Once your funds arrive, go to Buy or Trade, search for ADA or Cardano, enter the amount you want to spend, and click Buy. Your ADA will appear in your exchange balance within seconds.

📸 Screenshot: Exchange — Buy ADA screen Show the ADA buy form with an amount entered and the "Buy ADA" button
💡 Don't spend everything — you'll need a small amount left to cover the withdrawal fee in the next step (usually 1–2 ADA).
6

Copy your Lace wallet address

Open your Lace wallet extension, click Receive, and copy your full wallet address. It starts with addr1 and is very long — make sure you copy the whole thing.

📸 Screenshot: Lace wallet — Receive screen Show the receiving address and copy button in the Lace extension
7

Send a small test amount first

Go to Withdraw or Send on your exchange. Select ADA, select Cardano network, paste your wallet address, and enter a small amount (e.g. 5–10 ADA). Click send and wait for it to arrive in Lace — usually within a few minutes.

⚠️

Always send a small test amount first. Crypto transactions cannot be reversed. If you paste the wrong address, your ADA is gone permanently. A 5 ADA test costs almost nothing and could save everything.

📸 Screenshot: Exchange withdrawal screen Show the network selector (Cardano), address field, and amount — highlight the network selection
8

Confirm the test arrived, then send the rest

Open your Lace wallet and check your balance has gone up by the test amount. Once confirmed, go back to the exchange and withdraw your remaining ADA the same way.

✅

Your ADA is now in your own wallet. It's no longer on the exchange — you are in full control. Nobody can freeze it, take it, or block your access.

📸 Screenshot: Lace wallet showing received ADA balance Show the updated balance in the Lace dashboard after the transfer

What's next?

You have ADA in your own wallet. Now let it start earning for you.
