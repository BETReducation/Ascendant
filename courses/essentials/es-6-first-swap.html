Make your first token swap

Use Minswap — a decentralised exchange built on Cardano — to swap ADA for another token. No middleman, no account required.

⏱ 5 min 🔑 Lace wallet with ADA required 💸 Small amount of ADA for fees
Home › Courses › Essentials › First swap
✅ What you'll do: Swap ADA for another Cardano token
⚠️ DEX fee: ~0.3% + ~1–2 ADA network fee
💡 No account needed: Your wallet is your login
⚠️

Before you swap: Token prices on a DEX can move quickly. Only swap amounts you're comfortable with, and be aware that the token you receive may go up or down in value. This is not financial advice.

1

Go to Minswap

Type minswap.org directly into your browser address bar. This is one of the most popular DEXes on Cardano. Do not click links to it from social media or emails.

📸 Screenshot: Minswap homepage Show the Minswap swap interface with the "Connect Wallet" button visible
2

Connect your Lace wallet

Click Connect Wallet in the top right corner. A popup will appear listing available wallets — click Lace. Your Lace extension will open and ask you to approve the connection. Click Authorize.

📸 Screenshot: Minswap wallet connection popup Show the wallet selector with Lace highlighted
💡 Connecting your wallet to a dApp does NOT give it access to your funds. It only allows the site to see your address and request transactions that you must still manually approve.
3

Select what you want to swap

You'll see two token fields — From (what you're giving) and To (what you're getting). The top field defaults to ADA. Click the bottom field to choose a token to receive. For your first swap, try a well-known token like SNEK, MIN, or DJED.

📸 Screenshot: Minswap — token selector popup Show the token search field with a list of available tokens
4

Enter the amount and review the details

Type the amount of ADA you want to swap in the top field. Minswap will calculate how much of the other token you'll receive. Before confirming, check:

  • Price impact — keep this below 1% for normal swaps. High impact means you're getting a worse rate.
  • Minimum received — this is the worst case due to slippage.
  • DEX fee — usually around 0.3% plus ~2 ADA network fee.
📸 Screenshot: Minswap — swap form filled in with fee breakdown visible Show an example swap (e.g. 10 ADA → SNEK) with price impact and fee details expanded
5

Click Swap and confirm in Lace

Click the Swap button. Your Lace wallet will open automatically showing the transaction details — the amount of ADA leaving, the network fee, and what you'll receive. Read through it, then click Confirm and enter your wallet password.

📸 Screenshot: Lace transaction confirmation for the swap Show the Lace popup with the transaction summary — ADA amount, fee, and confirm button
✅

Your swap is submitted. Cardano transactions confirm in 20–60 seconds. The new token will appear in your Lace wallet shortly. You can track the transaction on cardanoscan.io.
