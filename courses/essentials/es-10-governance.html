Cast your governance vote

Cardano is governed on-chain by ADA holders. Here's how to register, delegate your vote, and actually participate in shaping the network.

⏱ 5 min 🗳 ADA in your wallet required 🌍 Your vote counts proportionally to your stake
Home › Courses › Essentials › Governance vote
🗳 What you'll do: Register and cast a vote on a governance action
🏛 Where: gov.tools — Cardano's official governance portal
💡 What it costs: ~0.17 ADA registration fee (one-time)
💡

What is Cardano governance? Under the Voltaire era, ADA holders vote on-chain to approve or reject governance actions — changes to the network, treasury spending, and protocol parameters. Your voting power is proportional to how much ADA you hold or have delegated.

1

Go to gov.tools

Type gov.tools directly into your browser. This is Cardano's official governance portal. Connect your Lace wallet by clicking Connect Wallet in the top right.

📸 Screenshot: gov.tools homepage Show the governance portal with the Connect Wallet button and dashboard overview
2

Register as a voter (one-time, ~0.17 ADA)

To participate in governance, you need to register on-chain first. Click Register or Become a voter. Lace will open with a small registration transaction. Confirm it — this is a one-time step.

📸 Screenshot: gov.tools — voter registration screen Show the registration prompt and the Lace transaction confirmation
3

Option A — Delegate your vote to a DRep

A DRep (Delegated Representative) is someone who votes on your behalf. If you don't want to research every governance action yourself, delegating to a DRep whose values you align with is a great option. Go to DReps, browse the list, and click Delegate on one you trust.

📸 Screenshot: gov.tools — DRep list Show the DRep directory with names, voting power, and delegate button
4

Option B — Vote directly on a proposal

Go to Governance Actions to see all live proposals. Each one shows a title, description, and voting deadline. Click a proposal to read the full details, then click Vote Yes, Vote No, or Abstain. Confirm in Lace.

📸 Screenshot: gov.tools — Governance action detail page Show a live proposal with the Yes/No/Abstain buttons and voting deadline
✅

Your vote is recorded on-chain. You can change it before the voting period closes. Your voting power is proportional to your ADA — every holder's voice counts.
