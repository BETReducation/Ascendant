Add a hardware wallet

A Ledger or Trezor is the gold standard for protecting your ADA. Connect one to Lace in a few minutes and your security goes to a different level.

⏱ 8 min 🛒 Requires a Ledger or Trezor device 🔒 Maximum self-custody security
Home › Courses › Essentials › Hardware wallet
🔒 What you'll have: Hardware-secured Cardano wallet
🛒 What you'll need: A Ledger Nano S Plus, Nano X, or Trezor Model T
💡 Why bother: Even if your computer is hacked, your ADA stays safe
💡

Why a hardware wallet? With a software wallet (like Lace), your private key lives on your computer. If someone installs malware on your device, they could access it. A hardware wallet stores your key on a separate physical device — transactions must be physically approved on the device itself.

1

Buy a Ledger — from the official site only

Go to ledger.com and buy a Nano S Plus (most affordable, works great) or Nano X (has Bluetooth). Never buy a hardware wallet from Amazon, eBay, or any third party — a pre-owned device could have been tampered with.

⚠️

Only buy from ledger.com directly. A tampered hardware wallet is one of the few ways someone can steal ADA even from a hardware wallet setup.

📸 Screenshot: ledger.com product page Show the Nano S Plus product listing on the official Ledger website
2

Set up your Ledger device

When your Ledger arrives, plug it into your computer via USB. Follow the on-screen instructions on the device itself to:

  • Set a PIN code (you'll enter this every time you use the device)
  • Write down your 24-word recovery phrase — this is separate from your Lace phrase
  • Confirm your recovery phrase on the device
📸 Photo: Ledger Nano S Plus device showing setup screen Show the physical device display during initial setup
3

Install Ledger Live and the Cardano app

Download Ledger Live from ledger.com/ledger-live. Open it, connect your Ledger, and go to My Ledger. Search for Cardano ADA and click Install. This installs the Cardano app onto your device.

📸 Screenshot: Ledger Live — My Ledger, installing Cardano ADA app Show the app catalogue with Cardano ADA and the Install button
4

Connect your Ledger to Lace

Open your Lace wallet extension. Click Connect hardware wallet. Select Ledger. Make sure your Ledger is plugged in and the Cardano app is open on the device. Lace will detect it and display your hardware wallet address.

📸 Screenshot: Lace — Hardware wallet connection screen Show the Ledger detected state in Lace with the wallet address shown
✅

You now have a hardware-secured wallet address. Send your ADA to this new address to fully protect it. Any transaction from this wallet must be physically approved by pressing a button on your Ledger device.

5

Move your ADA to your hardware wallet address

Copy your new Ledger wallet address from Lace (it starts with addr1). Go to your existing software wallet or exchange and send your ADA to this new address. As always, test with a small amount first.

📸 Screenshot: Lace — Receiving address for the Ledger wallet Show the Receive screen in Lace displaying the hardware wallet address
🔒

Your ADA is now protected by your hardware device. Even if your computer is completely compromised, an attacker cannot move your funds without physically pressing the button on your Ledger.
