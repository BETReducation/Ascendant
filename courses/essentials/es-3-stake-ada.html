Stake your ADA in 3 clicks

Put your ADA to work. Delegate to a staking pool and earn approximately 3–4% annually — with no lock-up and no risk to your ADA.

⏱ 5 min 🔓 Your ADA stays liquid 🏦 ADA in your Lace wallet required
Home › Courses › Essentials › Stake your ADA
✅ What you'll have: ADA earning staking rewards
📅 First rewards: After 2 Cardano epochs (~10 days)
🔓 No risk: Your ADA never leaves your wallet
💡

How staking works: You're not sending your ADA anywhere. You're simply telling the network "I want this pool to represent my stake." Your ADA stays in your wallet, fully spendable, while rewards accumulate every 5 days.

1

Open Lace and go to Staking

Click the Lace extension icon in your browser toolbar to open your wallet. In the left sidebar, click Staking. You'll see a list of available staking pools.

📸 Screenshot: Lace wallet — Staking section Show the Staking tab in the left sidebar and the pool list
2

Choose a staking pool

You'll see pools listed with their ticker name, saturation level, and estimated returns (ROS). Here's what to look for:

  • Saturation below 100% — over-saturated pools earn less. Aim for 50–90%.
  • ROS around 3–5% — this is the estimated annual return.
  • Pledge > 0 — pool operators who stake their own ADA are more committed.
  • Low or zero margin fee — this is the cut the pool takes before distributing rewards.
💡 You can search for a specific pool by its ticker. Community favourites include pools run by Cardano single pool operators — smaller pools that support decentralisation.
📸 Screenshot: Lace — Pool details panel Show a selected pool with saturation, ROS, margin fee, and pledge visible
3

Click "Stake" and confirm

Click on a pool to select it, then click Stake to this pool. Lace will show you a confirmation screen with a small transaction fee (around 0.17–2 ADA for the one-time registration). Enter your wallet password and click Confirm.

📸 Screenshot: Lace — Staking confirmation screen Show the pool name, fee breakdown, and the Confirm button
✅

Done. Your ADA is now delegated. Rewards start accumulating from the next epoch boundary. You'll receive your first rewards after approximately 10 days, then every 5 days after that — automatically, to your wallet.

4

Check your rewards

You can track your staking rewards in the Lace wallet under Staking → Your rewards. Rewards are added to your wallet automatically each epoch — no action needed from you.

📸 Screenshot: Lace — Staking rewards view Show the current delegation and accumulated rewards display
💡

You can change pools at any time with no penalty. You can spend your ADA at any time. Staking in Cardano has zero lock-up period — unlike many other blockchains.
