Add liquidity to a pool

Deposit a pair of tokens into a Minswap liquidity pool and earn a share of trading fees. Here's the step-by-step — including what impermanent loss means in plain English.

⏱ 5 min 🪙 You'll need two tokens (e.g. ADA + another) ⚠️ Read the risks section before proceeding
Home › Courses › Essentials › Add liquidity
💧 What you'll do: Provide liquidity and earn trading fees
⚠️ Risk: Impermanent loss if token prices diverge
🔓 Exit: Remove liquidity at any time
⚠️

Impermanent loss in one sentence: If the price ratio between your two tokens changes after you deposit, you could end up with less total value than if you'd simply held them. The bigger the price movement, the bigger the loss. Fees can offset this — but not always.

1

Go to Minswap and open the Liquidity page

Go to minswap.org and connect your Lace wallet. In the top navigation, click Liquidity (or Earn → Liquidity Pools). You'll see a list of all available pools.

📸 Screenshot: Minswap — Liquidity pools page Show the pool list with TVL, APR, and volume columns visible
2

Find a pool and click "Add Liquidity"

Search for the pair you want to provide — for example ADA/DJED (stablecoin pair, lower impermanent loss risk) or ADA/MIN. Click the pool to open its details, then click Add Liquidity.

💡 Stablecoin pairs (like ADA/DJED) have lower impermanent loss risk because one asset doesn't move in price. A good starting point for beginners.
📸 Screenshot: Minswap — Pool detail page with "Add Liquidity" button Show the ADA/DJED pool page with APR, TVL, and the Add Liquidity button
3

Enter your amounts

You must deposit both tokens in the correct ratio (set by the current pool price). Enter the amount of one token — Minswap will automatically calculate how much of the other you need. Make sure you have enough of both in your wallet.

📸 Screenshot: Minswap — Add liquidity form with both amounts filled Show the two token input fields auto-calculating based on one entry
4

Confirm and sign in Lace

Click Add Liquidity. Your Lace wallet opens — review the transaction, confirm the amounts, and click Confirm. The transaction will go on-chain within seconds.

In return, you receive LP tokens (liquidity provider tokens). These represent your share of the pool. Keep them — you need them to withdraw your liquidity later.

📸 Screenshot: Lace — Liquidity transaction confirmation Show the Lace popup confirming the deposit transaction
✅

You're now a liquidity provider. Every time someone swaps in this pool, you earn a proportional share of the 0.3% trading fee. Check your earnings on the Minswap dashboard.
