Set up your Cardano wallet

Download the Lace wallet, create your account, and secure your seed phrase. You'll be done in under 10 minutes.

⏱ 10 min 🖥 Desktop browser required 💰 Free — no ADA needed
Home › Courses › Essentials › Set up your wallet
✅ What you'll have: A working Cardano wallet
🔑 What you'll need: A computer and a pen and paper
⚠️ Important: Do not skip the seed phrase step
1

Go to the official Lace website

Open your browser and type lace.io directly into the address bar. Do not search for it on Google and click the first ad — scammers pay to appear at the top with fake wallet sites.

💡 Check the URL is exactly lace.io — not lace-wallet.com, lace-app.io, or anything else.
📸 Screenshot: lace.io homepage Show the official Lace homepage with the "Download" button visible
2

Click "Download Lace" and install the extension

Lace works as a browser extension. Click the download button — it will take you to the Chrome Web Store or Firefox Add-ons. Click Add to Chrome (or Add to Firefox), then Add extension when the popup appears.

📸 Screenshot: Chrome Web Store — Lace Wallet extension page Show the "Add to Chrome" button and the Input Output developer name
💡

Verify the developer listed is Input Output — the company behind Cardano's development. This confirms it's the real extension.

3

Pin the extension to your browser toolbar

Click the puzzle piece icon (🧩) in your browser toolbar, find Lace in the list, and click the pin icon. This keeps Lace easily accessible whenever you need it.

📸 Screenshot: Browser extensions menu with Lace pinned Show the pin icon next to Lace in the Chrome extensions dropdown
4

Click "Create a new wallet"

Click the Lace icon in your toolbar to open it. You'll see two options: Create a new wallet or Restore a wallet. Click Create a new wallet.

📸 Screenshot: Lace onboarding screen — Create vs Restore Show the two option buttons with "Create a new wallet" highlighted
5

Set a wallet password

Choose a strong password — at least 12 characters, mixing letters, numbers, and symbols. This password encrypts your wallet on this device. You'll need it every time you open Lace.

⚠️

This password protects your wallet on this device only. It is NOT a backup — that comes in the next step. If you forget this password you can still restore your wallet using your seed phrase.

📸 Screenshot: Lace — Set wallet password screen Show the password and confirm password fields
6

Write down your 24-word seed phrase — right now

Lace will show you 24 words in a specific order. These words are the only backup of your wallet. Get a pen and paper and write every word down, numbered 1–24, exactly as shown.

🔐

Never take a photo or screenshot of these words. Never type them into a notes app, email, or text message. Paper only. Anyone who sees these words can take all your ADA — permanently.

📸 Screenshot: Lace — 24-word seed phrase screen Show the grid of numbered words (words can be blurred/redacted for the example)
7

Confirm your seed phrase

Lace will ask you to select several of your words in order to confirm you wrote them down. This is a verification step — it won't proceed until you get them right. If you struggle, check your paper.

📸 Screenshot: Lace — Seed phrase verification screen Show the word selection grid where the user confirms specific words
8

You're done — your wallet is ready

Once verified, your Lace wallet is set up. You'll see your wallet dashboard with a receiving address starting with addr1. This is your public address — safe to share when someone wants to send you ADA.

📸 Screenshot: Lace — Main wallet dashboard Show the wallet home screen with 0 ADA balance and the wallet address visible
✅

Your wallet is set up. Store your seed phrase paper somewhere physically safe — a drawer, a safe, or split between two trusted locations. You're ready to receive ADA.
