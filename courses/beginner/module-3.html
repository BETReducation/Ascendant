🔑 Custodial vs non-custodial

This is the most important concept in all of crypto, so pay attention. There are two ways to hold ADA:

The single most important choice in crypto ownership.

🔐

"Not your keys, not your coins" — this is a well-known phrase in crypto for a reason. If an exchange goes bust, freezes withdrawals, or gets hacked, your ADA could be gone. A non-custodial wallet means only you can access your funds.

⬇️ Installing Lace — step by step

Follow these steps carefully. This process generates your wallet — the seed phrase you receive is the most important thing you'll handle in this entire course.

Installing & creating your Lace wallet
1
Go to the official website only: lace.io — never search for wallet extensions on Google and click the first result. Scammers buy ads to fake wallet sites to steal your seed phrase.
2
Install the browser extension from the Chrome Web Store or Firefox Add-ons. Verify the developer is listed as Input Output and the extension has thousands of reviews.
3
Click "Create a new wallet" and set a strong spending password. This password encrypts your wallet on this device — it's separate from your seed phrase.
4
Write down your 24-word seed phrase on paper — not in a notes app, not in a screenshot, not in email. This is covered in the next section.
5
Verify your seed phrase — Lace will ask you to confirm several words in order. This ensures you've written them down correctly before you can proceed.

📝 Your seed phrase — the most critical step

Your 24-word seed phrase (also called a recovery phrase or mnemonic) is the master key to your wallet. Anyone who has these words has complete access to your ADA — forever.

How to store your seed phrase safely
✓
Write it on paper — use a pen, write clearly, number each word. Then store this paper somewhere physically secure (a safe, a lockbox, or split between two trusted locations).
✓
Consider a steel backup — paper can burn or get wet. Steel seed phrase storage cards (like Cryptosteel or Billdodl) are fireproof and waterproof. Worth it for larger amounts.
✗
Never store it digitally — no photos, no text files, no emails, no cloud notes, no password managers. If it's connected to the internet, it can be stolen.
✗
Never share it with anyone — no customer support, no "Cardano Foundation" DMs on Twitter, no giveaway verification. Legitimate services will never ask for your seed phrase.

✓ Benefits of self-custody

  • Full control — no one can freeze your funds
  • Access DeFi, dApps, and NFT marketplaces
  • Stake natively and earn full rewards
  • Not affected if exchanges fail
  • Your ADA survives as long as your seed phrase does

✗ Responsibilities

  • You are solely responsible for your seed phrase
  • No customer support if you lose access
  • Lost seed phrase = lost ADA, permanently
  • You must stay alert to phishing and scams
  • Steeper learning curve than an exchange
